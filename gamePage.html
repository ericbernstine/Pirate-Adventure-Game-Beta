Welcome to the Pirate Game, Captain

INTRO: In this game, you aim to claim a bounty on a dangerous creature. You will first need to pick the creature you want to hunt. Each creature will have its stats listed on the bottom left of each image. Your objective is to build a ship that can take out the monster you hope to best. To start, you will have 30,000 gold. A ship, Pirate Crew, Captain, and Cannons will all need to be bought and paid for. Each of these options will have their stats listed on the bottom left of each image.

HOW TO PLAY: Scroll by clicking the arrow on the right side of each image. To make a selection, press “Click Here” on any image, confirm your selection and continue scrolling. BE CAREFUL with selections you will only be able to make one per category. Be sure not to spend too much or too little, aim to have your stats better than the monster you’re hunting. Display your stats with the button above the images.

RESET: If you make a selection you didn't mean to, click the Home Page button and start again

Good luck Captain !!
